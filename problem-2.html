Where are the role models?

Kids are aspire to be what they see around them. How can we ask our next generation of females to pursue tech careers when we can't provide them with female role model examples currently in the industry. Immediately, male tech entrepreneurs spring to mind, such as Mark Zuckerberg and Bill Gates. However if asked to name female one, our minds blank. The video from NPR articulates this idea brilliantly.
Here are 10 role models for you to research more about:

  1. Sheryl Sandberg: COO of Facebook
  2. Ada Lovelace: World's first ever computer programmer
  3. Gina Trapani: Built projects including Narrow the Gapp, Life Hacker and Makerbase
  4. Karen Spärck Jones: Inspired Google
  5. Ginni Rometty: CEO of IBM
  6. Hadley Beeman: NHS Chief Technology Advisor
  7. The Women of ENIAC
  8. Grace Hopper: "The mother of Computing
  9. Annie Easley: Hybrid Car Battery developer
  10. Adele Goldberg: Inspired the first Apple Computer
