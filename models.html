The Problem

Where are our role models?

Kids aspire to be what they see around them. How can we ask our next generation of females to pursue tech careers when we can't provide them with female role model examples currently in the industry.Immediately, male tech entrepreneurs spring to mind, such as Mark Zuckerberg and Bill Gates.

However if asked to name one female, our minds blank. The video below from NPR articulates this idea brilliantly!
